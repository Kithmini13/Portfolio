Bank Loan Report Dashboard Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of loan applications, funding performance, and key financial metrics, helping stakeholders monitor and evaluate bank loan operations efficiently.

Key Highlights:
- Total Loan Applications: 38.6K (up 6.9% MoM)
- Total Funded Amount: $435.8M
- Total Amount Received: $473.1M
- Average Interest Rate: 12.0%
- Average DTI (Debt-to-Income): 13.3%

Dashboard Sections:

1. Summary View
- Offers a high-level snapshot of loan quality.
- Good Loans: 86.2% of total applications, with $435.8M received.
- Bad Loans: 13.8%, representing $65.5M in funding.
- Detailed breakdown of loan statuses (Charged Off, Current, Fully Paid).

2. Overview Tab
- Visual insights into trends and distributions:
- Monthly Application Trends
- Loan Purpose Distribution (e.g., Debt Consolidation dominates with 18K applications)
- Applications by Home Ownership, Term Length, and Employee Tenure
- Geographic Spread of Applications

3. Details Tab
- Tabular view of individual loan data.
- Shows specifics like:
- Loan purpose, amount, interest rate, installment
- Homeownership type, grade/subgrade
- Issue dates and amount received

Utility:
Perfect for analysts, managers, or executives to:
- Track performance metrics
- Assess credit risk
- Identify trends by borrower type and loan use
- Make data-driven decisions
